Great Battle Under the Sea – Big Fish Eats Small Fish 4.0

Welcome to Deep Hunter

Deep Hunter opens up a vibrant ocean world where players embark on an epic journey of survival, evolution, and conquest. Dive into real-time battles, transform into powerful sea creatures, and compete for dominance in the mysterious depths!

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Choose Your Creature: Start as one of three species: Tigerfish (high speed, dash skill), Electric Jellyfish (stun ability), or Sea Turtle (high armor, abundant stamina).
  • Real-Time Map: Navigate a dynamic ocean map inspired by Slither.io, with improved mechanics and live PvP action.
  • PvP & PvE: Battle other players, hunt prey, and avoid becoming food for larger predators. Transform into new forms as you grow and survive.

Survival & Evolution

  • HP System: Manage your health points—taking damage from predators or hazards reduces HP.
  • Stamina: Actions like dashing or special skills consume stamina, which regenerates over time or with items.
  • Transformation: As you consume prey and survive, evolve into stronger forms with new abilities and appearances.

Deep Sea Boss Events

Face off against colossal Deep Sea Bosses in special timed events. Team up with others or challenge bosses solo for rare rewards and leaderboard glory!

Ocean Economy

  • Token Rewards: Earn DH coins by defeating enemies, surviving events, and completing missions.
  • Coin-to-Token Conversion: Exchange in-game DH coins for blockchain tokens at a fixed rate (see whitepaper for details).
  • Marketplace: Trade items, skins, and power-ups with other players in a secure ocean economy.

NFT & Blockchain Integration

  • Unique Skins & Skills: Collect and trade NFT-based fish skins, special abilities, and rare items.
  • Web3 Security: Enjoy secure, transparent transactions and true ownership of your digital assets.

Roadmap to Launch
